# I’ll Do It 🇿🇦

## Master Vision & Platform Blueprint

---

# The Idea

“I’ll Do It” is a South African work and hustle platform that connects ordinary people with opportunities to earn money through skills, services, quick tasks, freelance work, and local jobs.

The platform is designed for:

* freelancers
* students
* hustlers
* side-income earners
* unemployed youth
* small businesses
* local communities

Unlike global platforms that focus mainly on international remote work, “I’ll Do It” is built around South African realities:

* local payments
* mobile-first usage
* trust and verification
* low-data experiences
* nearby services
* township and community economies
* practical day-to-day work

This is not just another freelancer app.

It is a digital hustle ecosystem.

---

# Exact MVP Design

## MVP Philosophy

The first version of “I’ll Do It” should feel:

* fast
* useful
* trustworthy
* simple
* alive

The MVP is not meant to impress investors.
It is meant to prove people will actually use the platform.

The goal of Version 1 is simple:

A person can:

1. create an account
2. offer a service
3. find work
4. message someone
5. complete a job
6. receive payment

If these six things work smoothly, the platform already has value.

---

# MVP Scope

## What The MVP WILL Include

### Authentication

* email signup
* Google login
* phone number verification
* username creation
* profile setup

---

### User Profiles

Each user gets:

* profile picture
* display name
* bio
* skills
* province/location
* ratings
* completed jobs
* verification status

---

### Service Listings

Workers can create service cards.

Example:

* “I will design your logo”
* “I will tutor mathematics”
* “I will edit your TikTok videos”

Each listing includes:

* title
* description
* category
* price
* delivery time
* gallery images

---

### Job Requests

Clients can post jobs.

Example:

* “Need a logo for my clothing brand”
* “Need help fixing Wi-Fi”

Fields:

* title
* description
* budget
* category
* deadline
* optional images

---

### Messaging System

Core feature.

Support:

* real-time chat
* image sharing
* voice notes
* typing indicators
* delivery/read status

Messaging should feel instant.

---

### Search & Discovery

Users can search by:

* category
* location
* price
* verified status
* ratings

---

### Ratings & Reviews

After every completed job:

* client rates worker
* worker rates client

Simple 5-star system.

---

### Notifications

Push notifications for:

* new messages
* job accepted
* payment updates
* review received

---

### Wallet System

Simple wallet initially.

Functions:

* pending earnings
* available balance
* withdrawal requests
* payment history

---

# What The MVP WILL NOT Include Yet

Do NOT build yet:

* AI recommendations
* video calling
* live maps
* advanced analytics
* team accounts
* subscriptions
* agency systems
* full escrow automation
* social feed
* advanced moderation AI

These come later.
